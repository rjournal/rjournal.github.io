\title{ggdensity: Improved Bivariate Density Visualization in R}


\author{by James Otto and David Kahle}

\maketitle

\abstract{%
The \href{https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=ggdensity}{ggdensity} R package extends the functionality of \href{https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=ggplot2}{ggplot2} by providing more interpretable visualizations of bivariate density estimates using highest density regions (HDRs). The visualizations are created via drop-in replacements for the standard \href{https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=ggplot2}{ggplot2} functions used for this purpose: geom\_hdr() for geom\_density\_2d\_filled() and geom\_hdr\_lines() for geom\_density\_2d(). These new geoms improve on those of \href{https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=ggplot2}{ggplot2} by communicating the probabilities associated with the displayed regions. Various statistically rigorous estimators are available, as well as convenience functions geom\_hdr\_fun() and geom\_hdr\_fun\_lines() for plotting HDRs of user-specified probability density functions. Associated geoms for rug plots and pointdensity scatterplots are also presented.
}
